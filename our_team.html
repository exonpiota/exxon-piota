Meet the Team

Dr. Angel Davila, DC

Dr. Angel is a passionate advocate for helping individuals live life to the fullest. With a specialty in treating active people, he is dedicated to empowering his patients to enjoy all that life has to offer. Growing up, Dr. Angel had a deep love for sports and fitness, which inspired him to pursue a Bachelor of Science in Kinesiology from Louisiana State University. He then went on to earn a Doctor of Chiropractic and a Master’s in Strength and Human Performance from Parker University in Dallas, Texas. He is committed to guiding his patients, whether they are seasoned athletes or looking to kickstart a healthier lifestyle, towards achieving their full potential.

Dr. Kyler Brown, DC

Dr. Kyler Brown specializes in pre/post surgical protocols, sports injuries and bridging the gap from rehab to full activity. Dr. Brown moved to Austin in 2008 and since then has enjoyed seeing patients of all walks of life while raising his family.  As a former athlete, he understands the importance of a team approach and urgency to get people back on track as quickly as possible.

Dr. Jordan Pellien, DC

Dr. Jordan was born and raised in Austin, he graduated from Westlake High School and attended the University of Texas where he was a member of the Longhorn baseball team. He currently provides chiropractic care to both Texas Longhorn football and baseball teams. In these environments Dr. Pellien has worked side by side with multiple practitioners and knows how integration can make a huge impact.

Sharese Douglas, PTA

Sharese specializes in strength and conditioning. With a background as a Physical Therapist Assistant, and over a decade of strength training experience, she loves helping clients get stronger. Sharese is experienced in both navigating around injuries and working to prevent them. She enjoys working with clients of all different experience levels. Sharese is a mother of two young kiddos, is often embarking on a new house project, and loves to help people experience the positive effects of getting stronger.

Lauren Brown, LMT
Lauren’s integrated massage utilizes deep tissue therapy, fascial stretching & release to improve your movement potential. As a lifelong athlete and yoga teacher, she understands the movement demands and mindset of her clients. In her free time, she is enjoying playtime outside with her dog, traveling, or spending time experimenting in the kitchen.



ABOUT US

WHAT WE DO

We help companies become more profitable by reducing their cost, increasing efficiencies, strengthening revenues, and ultimately improving their profitability. Our risk free business model typical yields between 10% to 20% profitability to our clients.

TARGETED CUSTOMERS

Our target clients are businesses that are categorized as small to midsize. These include, but are not exclusive to businesses that may not have the resources or supply chain capabilities to deliver the desired year-over-year cost improvements required to maintain or increase a competitive edge within their respective industry.

BUSINESS MODEL

Our primary focus is to deliver bottom line results to our clients. With a high level of confidence in achieving this objective, we provide our services at no cost or risk to the client. Upon successful delivery of the cost improvements, we will share in a percentage of the savings.
